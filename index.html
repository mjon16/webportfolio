Programming Basics

HTML, or HyperText Markup Language, is the fundamental building block of the web. As a web developer, I leverage HTML to structure content, ensuring a solid foundation for websites. With its simple yet powerful tags, HTML enables me to create organized and accessible pages, laying the groundwork for seamless user experiences. From headings to paragraphs, links to images, HTML forms the backbone of the online world, allowing me to bring ideas to life and deliver information in a clear, standardized format.

Cascading Style Sheets (CSS) is my creative tool in the web development toolbox. With CSS, I breathe life into HTML, styling and beautifying elements to create visually stunning and responsive websites. By leveraging Bootstrap, I enhance both the aesthetics and functionality of websites, providing a solid foundation for an exceptional user experience.
